About

What drives me: Working with people I love to spend time with, feel like I can grow, learn with, be proud of, and respect. Value and mission alignment. Product that actually works, helps people, and respects society.

Professional Journey

I stared my career at an SF start-up, which has informed my builder's ethos for my entire career. Since then, I’ve worked across data companies of all sizes, learning how to operate within large corporate environments while maintaining a problem solver's mindset

I got a big break working on a research project intersection of ML and sports fandom at USC. That opportunity led me to New York City, where I helped build a data visualization practice and later expanded my work into data infrastructure and privacy-focused products.

Most recently, I worked at Samba TV in Taiwan, a San Francisco–based technology company specializing in TV viewership data. There, I partnered with SoC manufacturers and TV OS/OEMs to integrate and innovate on automatic content recognition and AI capabilities at the chipset level. Prior to that role, I completed a joint Executive MBA program run by UCLA and the National University of Singapore with a focus on technology and Asia, gaining a higher level of emotional intelligence and understanding how to work with institutional differences across regions.
